About Me

Hey there! My name is Paul Zavattieri and I'm currently studying the Full-Stack Web Development curriculum at UNC Coding Boot Camp. I live in Raleigh, NC and I graduated from NC State University in December 2014 with a degree in finance. After working in the finance industry for a couple years, I decided to make a change and pursue my passion for web development. I strive to master multiple programming languages and use my skills to design appealing web pages and dynamic web applications.

In addition to developing, I also enjoy traveling, craft breweries, sports, and live music. Please check out my portfolio to see what I've been up to and feel free to drop me a line. Thanks for visiting!
